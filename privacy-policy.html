Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

AccountingAI ("the App") is a private, internal application developed for and used exclusively by our accounting firm. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data accessed through QuickBooks Online.

2. Data We Access

The App accesses QuickBooks Online accounting data including customer records, invoices, transactions, and financial reports solely for the purpose of providing internal accounting assistance to authorized firm personnel.

3. How We Use Data

Data accessed from QuickBooks is used exclusively to:

— Answer internal accounting questions posed by authorized firm staff

— Suggest transaction categorizations for review and approval

— Generate internal business proposals and documents

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share any QuickBooks data or client information with any third parties. Data is not used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose outside of internal accounting operations.

5. Data Storage

The App does not permanently store QuickBooks data. Data is fetched in real time from QuickBooks Online when needed and is not retained in any external database. OAuth tokens used to access QuickBooks are stored securely on our private server and are not shared.

6. AI Processing

Accounting questions and relevant financial data may be processed by Anthropic's Claude AI API to generate responses. This processing is subject to Anthropic's data privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy. Anthropic does not use API data to train its models.

7. Security

The App is accessible only to authorized personnel via password-protected login. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. Access tokens are stored securely and never shared.

8. User Rights

As this is an internal tool, all users are authorized firm employees or principals. Any questions about data handling can be directed to the firm's administrator.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the firm administrator directly.
